The story of my journey as a working adult

I joined IBM fresh after completing my Diploma in Engineering Informatics from NYP. After working for about five years, I felt I was stable enough (financially as well as work-wise) and ready to take a part-time degree. I took a degree in Bachelor of Science in Business Information Systems and Cyber Forensics, Information Security & Management and completed it two and a half years later. Besides learning what was taught, I also learnt a few valuable life lessons while doing the part-time degree. I also got to make new friends, some became close and still keep in touch till today. After taking a long break from studying (and spending lots of time in front of the tv - after work, that is), I started studying again. This time, I took the Certified Ethical Hacker course, by EC-Council. A lot of things also happened at the same time. It was another experience that made me grow as a person. I realize that challenges make me a better person, and it is the people around me, family, colleagues, friends, teachers, who make it easier for me to get through tough times. But of course, I also turn to tv shows which are funny and inspiring to motivate myself! I also learn from the characters in the show about many aspects of life. That's why, I like to watch TV : )
I'd like to share them with others too, and hope that you will be inspired as well. Have Fun!
